The Girls

The Code 4 Cape Town site at Makapula Girls Secondary School consisted of many talented young women from both Kloetiesville High School and Makapula Secondary School, specifically selected for their drive, passion to make a difference and inteligence to tackle the problems one faces in the working world and in programming.

Over the course of their 6 months with Code4CT in 2016 they covered the basics if web development and programming with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. How to plan for and design ideas, not letting them just disappear. Most importantly opening their eyes to one of the most, if not the most, in demand job sectors with has a vast array of fields and specialisations to allow them to foolow their dreams and make a difference in the world.

About Code4CT

We want to see young women empowered to leverage technology for social innovation, so that there is greater diversity in the African tech industry. We want high school girls to have an enabling environment to imagine and create new technology that is relevant to a South African context.

We equip high potential female learners with the coding, problem solving and life skills that will allow them to leverage technology to contribute meaningfully to South Africaâ€™s innovation system. The program exposes young women to how technology can be used for social innovation and helps them navigate critical study decisions.
