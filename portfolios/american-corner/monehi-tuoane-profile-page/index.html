My Code Camp App

During Code Camp, me and 15 other girls, meet up with an organisaion call Femme, standing for 'Freedom of Education Motivates Empowerment'. What this Oganisation does, is that it goes to different places around South Africa, teaching young girls about the mestruaton cycle.

We made an app called 'Big Sista'. What this app does is that it informs the app user about the different cycles of menstruation, it informs them about the vigina and what happens in vigina during menstruation. It also informs the user about the different senitory products and tips about how to make your period less painfull. The app consists of a menstruation calculator, which calculates which menstruation cycle your on and will remind the user when their next period starts.

The app also consists of a quiz, where by the user can test how how much they know about menstruation. If you get any of the questions incorrect the app will provide you with a link that will have all the information about that spesific question/s that you got wrong.

To make the users feel more comfortable with the app, we made the app available in English, Afrikaans and in IsiXhosa.
