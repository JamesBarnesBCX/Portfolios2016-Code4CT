Students For A Better Future

Students For A Better Future is a scholarship progamme for the academically gifted and financially needy. But over the course of the 3 years that I've been with SBF it has shown me that it's not just a scholarship programme but a family and I've had the utmost pleasure to be apart of this family and meeting the wonderful people within it.

Outreach Work

I am extremely passionate about giving back to my community. I am involved in 3 different outreach programmes and it has taught me so much about life. They mainly focused on education and helping people with different backgrounds have access to tutoring, mentoring and interaction with girls like myself. One of my main motivations is the fact that i am a proud South African. The pride I have in my country is one of the reasons I try so hard to better it whether I'm working on improving education or encouraging people to recycle and keep our Earth clean.

Springfield Convent Girls School

I absolutely love school and all the doors it has opened for me. During the 3 years I've spent at Springfield I've really grown as a person and found myself. I found out who I am as a person, what i value and what I want out of life. It feels asif I havent really lived until high school. And in the course of 3 years I've learned to play 2 instruments, I;ve been part of a countless number of clubs, I've found my passion for reading and outreach and I made incredible relationships that will last a lifetime.

Code For Cape Town

I am extremely privileged and grateful to be a part of code4ct. If it wasn’t for code4ct I wouldn’t have met the amazing people that I did, formed the long-lasting bonds that I did and I wouldn’t have been able to code and discover the passion I have for coding. Coding has awakened a desire within me to create and change. I’ve always considered myself an uncreative person where arts and styles are concerned but coding has given me the opportunity to let my creative side free to explore, change and create. Coding has given me a whole new look on life and community. I’ve always been passionate about helping people but now that I’ve been taught a skill that can be so beneficial to people in need; I can’t help but get extra excited about coding and all my outreach work.
