About me

I am a 17-year-old aspiring entrepreneur, that loves to ponder on the unanswered questions of the world. My deep desire to travel and learn fuels me to push myself to new limits. I have 2 sisters and an imaginary dog named Zeus, which I hope will someday become real. Like Dora, I too am an explorer, who hopes to someday explore unknown sectors of the world.

I am Currently a participant of Code4CT,a program that equips young girls with coding skills. Code4CT has given me the confidence to undertake other challenges and coding has now become something I do for fun. I try to utilise this confidence in every aspect of my life whether it is in the process of launching my own startup or attending networking events within Cape Town.

Technology has become an essential part of our lives, and is utilized in almost every industry.I have had many passions and intrests in my life and technology connects them all. Code4CT gives me the foundation I need to pursue a career in technology and maybe even create new innovative technology!
